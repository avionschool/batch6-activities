FAQS

1. WHAT KIND OF TEA DO YOU USE?

We are committed in discovering the best teas in Asia. Each season, Happy-Tea-Center selects only the finest whole leaf tea using the most stringent selection criteria. After being harvested, these fresh, handpicked leaves undergo the traditional 12 stages of tea making procedures, from first stage of wilting to last stage of curing.

2. WHERE DO YOU SOURCE YOUR FRUITS?

Our fruits are sourced from the best locations, both local and abroad. Happy-Tea-Center is recognized for adopting a global procurement policy in terms of sourcing the best seasonal fruits and other primary ingredients to ensure the quality of its products.

3. HOW LONG WILL MY DRINK STAY FRESH?

To ensure quality and freshness, we recommend consuming your drink within two (2) hours.

4. ARE YOU OPEN FOR FRANCHISE?

We are currently not open for any franchising opportunities in the Philippines.
